Southbank Wintertime Market


Situated alongside the Thames, take in the sights, sounds and smells of Southbank Centre as it is transformed into a thriving winter market containing hot food (including cheese themed pop-up “Bar Under the Bridge”), hand-made festive gifts and family shows such as “Rumpelstiltskin” and “Circus 1903”.


Address: Queens Walk, SE1 8XX     Website: southbankcentre.co.uk

Winter Wonderland


First set up in 2006, Hyde Park’s Winter Wonderland has so much to offer. Look over central London by going up on the giant wheel, hone your ice-sculpting talents by visiting one of the workshops, or head to the MegaDome for a glimpse of some death-defying feats at the all new show “Cirque Beserk”.


Address: Hyde Park Corner, W1J 7NT     Website: hydeparkwinterwonderland.com

Winterville


Situated south of the city in Clapham Common, you’ll find some more alternative ways to have some festive fun! Take yourself back to the 80s with a thrilling roller disco, or feel free to watch some Christmas classics showing at the Backyard Cinema. Why not challenge your friends to a game of crazy golf at the Plonk Hole?


Address: Rookery Road, SW4 0RJ     Website: winterville.co.uk
