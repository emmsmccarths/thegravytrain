Winter Markets

Southbank Centre Wintertime Market

Line: Northern (also Jubilee, Bakerloo, Waterloo & City)
Station: Waterloo

Distance from tube: 5 minute walk

Situated alongside the Thames, take in the sights, sounds and smells of Southbank Centre as it is transformed into a thriving winter market containing hot food (including cheese themed pop-up “Bar Under the Bridge”), hand-made festive gifts and family shows such as “Rumpelstiltskin” and “Circus 1903”.

Hyde Park's Winter Wonderland

Line: Piccadilly
Stations: Hyde Park Corner, Knightsbridge

Distance from tube: 10 minute walk

First set up in 2006, Hyde Park’s Winter Wonderland has so much to offer. Look over central London by going up on the giant wheel, hone your ice-sculpting talents by visiting one of the workshops, or head to the MegaDome for a glimpse of some death-defying feats at the all new show “Cirque Beserk”.

Winterville

Line: Northern
Station: Clapham Common

Distance from tube: 10 minute walk

Situated south of the city in Clapham Common, you’ll find some more alternative ways to have some festive fun! Take yourself back to the 80s with a thrilling roller disco, or feel free to watch some Christmas classics showing at the Backyard Cinema. Why not challenge your friends to a game of crazy golf at the UV powered Plonk Hole?
